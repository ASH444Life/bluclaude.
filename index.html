Our Story

Where the Lake
Meets the Plate

Perched on the shore of Lake Michigan with panoramic views of Sleeping Bear Bay and the Manitou Islands, Blu offers a setting unparalleled by any Michigan restaurant. Founded by the Chamberlain family and now led by Chef Brandon Chamberlain, Blu blends European technique with the finest locally sourced ingredients.


Every menu is crafted daily — shaped by season, by what's freshest from Michigan's farms and waters, and by a deep respect for the landscape just outside our windows. DiRōNA award-winning, Travelers' Choice recognized, and beloved by guests from across the country.
